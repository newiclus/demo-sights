Student Data Tracking

Tests taken both in class and at home are automatically tracked in the Teacher’s Dashboard, providing at-a-glance progress monitoring.

Incremental Rehearsal

Students are tested using a mix of new words interspersed with previous-level words, a method proven to get students learning more total words.

List
Manager

Teachers can choose from Fry and Dolch lists or create custom lists. Lists are then assigned to classes and individual students.

Parent - Teacher connection

Never miss a beat: teachers see at-home practice test results; parents get their child’s newest words to study; students learn + retain sight words 3x faster.

Teachers get a FREE 10-Action Trial to try Sights risk-free before subscribing for $8/mo. During the trial you have access to everything Sights offers: adding students, taking tests, moving students up a level, setting up your List Manager, and more. The following count as one action each: moving a student UP or DOWN a level, taking a test (Dashboard or Parent App).

Once subscribed, just archive your Sights classroom when your sight word curriculum is finished, and your subscription will terminate and billing will stop. When you are ready to start a new school year with new classes, simply create a new classroom and set up a new billing cycle.
