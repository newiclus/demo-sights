Student Data Tracking

Tests taken both in class and at home are automatically tracked in the Teacher’s Dashboard, providing at-a-glance progress monitoring.

Incremental Rehearsal

Students are tested using a mix of new words interspersed with previous-level words, a method proven to get students learning more total words.

List
Manager

Teachers can choose from Fry and Dolch lists or create custom lists. Lists are then assigned to classes and individual students.

Parent - Teacher connection
